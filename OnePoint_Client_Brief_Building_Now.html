  • Voice-to-structured-record flow.

  • Low-tech-family optimized operations view.
  • Placement-to-care continuity workflow.

What We Are Building Now (Round 2)

The Core Product Loop

Step 1: Caregiver records a voice note after (or during) shift activity.
Step 2: System converts speech into structured records (vitals, meds, observations, notes).
Step 3: Team sees a clean, shared timeline and current on-duty context.
Step 4: AI generates shift handoff briefing with critical alerts + pending tasks.
Step 5: Export is available for legal/risk review and operational history.
Step 6: Placement intake can transition into ongoing care record without duplicate entry.

Design Principles

Voice-first Mobile-first Low-tech inclusive Legally defensible logs Single source of truth No duplicate entry
